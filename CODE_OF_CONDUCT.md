# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
We expect contributors to be respectful, collaborative, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Constructive feedback
- Respect for different viewpoints
- Professional communication

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Trolling or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent

## Scope
This applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests, and community discussions.

## Reporting
Report incidents to maintainers via the security contact in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments/commits/issues/PRs that violate this policy and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for repeated violations.
